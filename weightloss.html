⚖️ Weight Loss Guide

Burn Fat • Stay Healthy • Stay Consistent

🏃 Cardio

  • Brisk Walking – 30–45 Minutes
  • Jogging – 20–30 Minutes
  • Cycling – 30 Minutes
  • Skipping – 10–15 Minutes
  • HIIT – 15–20 Minutes (2–3 Times/Week)

💪 Strength Training

  • Push Day – 1 Day
  • Pull Day – 1 Day
  • Leg Day – 1 Day
  • Full Body Workout – Optional

🥗 Healthy Diet

  • Protein-rich foods (Paneer, Soya Chunks, Dal)
  • Whole Wheat Roti
  • Brown Rice or Moderate White Rice
  • Vegetables & Salads
  • Fresh Fruits
  • Drink 2.5–3 Litres of Water Daily

🚫 Avoid

  • Sugary Drinks
  • Junk Food
  • Deep Fried Foods
  • Too Many Sweets
  • Late Night Snacking

💡 Weight Loss Tips

  • Stay in a small calorie deficit.
  • Eat enough protein every day.
  • Sleep 7–9 hours.
  • Exercise consistently.
  • Track your progress weekly.
  • Be patient and trust the process.
