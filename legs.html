🟢 Leg Day Workout

Quads • Hamstrings • Glutes • Calves

🔥 Warm-Up

  • 5–10 minutes brisk walk or cycling
  • Bodyweight Squats × 15
  • Walking Lunges × 10 each leg
  • Hip Mobility Stretch

💪 Main Workout

  1. Barbell Squats
    4 Sets × 6–8 Reps

  2. Romanian Deadlifts
    3 Sets × 8–10 Reps

  3. Leg Press
    3 Sets × 10–12 Reps

  4. Walking Lunges
    3 Sets × 12 Steps Each Leg

  5. Leg Extension
    3 Sets × 12–15 Reps

  6. Leg Curl
    3 Sets × 12–15 Reps

  7. Standing Calf Raises
    4 Sets × 15–20 Reps

⏱ Rest Time

  • Heavy exercises: 2–3 minutes
  • Isolation exercises: 60–90 seconds

💡 Tips

  • Keep your back straight during squats.
  • Go through a full range of motion.
  • Control the weight on every rep.
  • Don't skip calf training.
  • Progressively increase weight over time.
