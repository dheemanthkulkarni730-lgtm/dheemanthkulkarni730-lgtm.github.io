🔵 Pull Day Workout

Back • Biceps • Rear Delts

🔥 Warm-Up

  • 5–10 minutes light cardio
  • Band pull-aparts
  • Arm circles
  • 2 light sets of Lat Pulldowns

💪 Main Workout

  1. Pull-Ups / Assisted Pull-Ups
    4 Sets × 6–10 Reps

  2. Lat Pulldown
    3 Sets × 8–12 Reps

  3. Barbell Bent-Over Row
    4 Sets × 8–10 Reps

  4. Seated Cable Row
    3 Sets × 10–12 Reps

  5. Face Pulls
    3 Sets × 12–15 Reps

  6. Barbell Bicep Curl
    3 Sets × 8–10 Reps

  7. Hammer Curl
    3 Sets × 10–12 Reps

⏱ Rest Time

  • Heavy compound exercises: 2–3 minutes
  • Isolation exercises: 60–90 seconds

💡 Tips

  • Keep your back straight during rows.
  • Pull with your elbows, not your hands.
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades at the top.
  • Lower the weight under control.
  • Focus on good form over heavy weight.
