🔴 Push Day Workout

Chest • Shoulders • Triceps

🔥 Warm-Up

  • 5–10 minutes light cardio
  • Shoulder mobility exercises
  • 2 light sets of Bench Press

💪 Main Workout

  1. Barbell Bench Press
    4 Sets × 6–8 Reps

  2. Incline Dumbbell Press
    3 Sets × 8–10 Reps

  3. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press
    3 Sets × 8–10 Reps

  4. Dumbbell Lateral Raises
    3 Sets × 12–15 Reps

  5. Cable Triceps Pushdown
    3 Sets × 10–12 Reps

  6. Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension
    3 Sets × 10–12 Reps

⏱ Rest Time

  • Heavy compound exercises: 2–3 minutes
  • Isolation exercises: 60–90 seconds

💡 Tips

  • Focus on proper form.
  • Increase weight gradually (progressive overload).
  • Stay hydrated throughout the workout.
  • Train close to failure while maintaining good technique.
